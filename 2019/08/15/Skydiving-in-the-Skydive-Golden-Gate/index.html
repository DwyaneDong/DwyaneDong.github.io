Skydiving in the Skydive Golden Gate

skydiving in the Skydive Golden Gate

On the 11, August, 2019, I went to the skydive Club alone which named Skydive Golden Gate in Novato.And I experienced the most exciting adventure ever in my life yet.

When I heard that my friends would let me go alone because of the fear, honestly, I was a little bit hesitated about this trip. Later in the evening, a thought came into my mind , life is a long-distance adventure, I would recall this decision and be inspired to go on when I face with some real adventure or trouble in the future.So I clicked the reservation button unshakenly.

On my way to the club, I meet a Nepalese Uber driver. We had a pleasant communication. I learned that he had came here America for three years and he had two little twins girls in Nepal.

-“you miss them so much,right?”

-“of course, I left them when they were only 6 years old, I am missing them every minute.”

-“how often do you come back per year?”

-“No, I haven’t been back for two years.The ticket is too expensive. I am here for making money rather than traveling.”

-“how long you will stay here?”

-“maybe 2 years, maybe 3 years. I must come back before my daughters are going to the junior high school.”

“hey,man. Life is struggle,right?”

“yeah…….life is struggle……life is struggle.”

…….
